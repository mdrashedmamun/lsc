# Visual Refinement: The "Capture" Metaphor

The current visual (a circular radar/targeting icon) is too clinical. It suggests "Scanning" or "Detecting," but it doesn't represent the **Emotional and Strategic Value** of capturing a founder's unique vision.

## 1. The Problem with the Current Visual
*   **Too Abstract:** A founder’s vision is vast and personal. A simple dot-and-circle feels like a GPS coordinate, not an "Extraction of Wisdom."
*   **Static:** It lacks the sense of *transformation* from thought to asset.

---

## 2. The Suggestion: "Crystallization of Vision"

Instead of a radar, we should use an **Architectural Extraction Diagram**. 

### The Visual Narrative:
Imagine a 3-layer vertical structure (Isometric):

1.  **The Ethereal Layer (Thoughts):** At the top, a cluster of soft, glowing, organic "cloud-like" paths or loose wireframes. This represents the raw, unstructured expertise in the founder's head.
2.  **The Extraction Pillar:** A vertical set of 0.5px "Drafting Lines" (the blueprint style) that connects the top cloud to the bottom structure. This represents your process of digging in and pulling out the gold.
3.  **The Systemic Base (The Stack):** At the bottom, a solid, glowing, isometric metallic cube or a series of stacked "Value Plates." This represents the final asset—the Leverage Stack.

---

## 3. Why this works for the Brand Promise:

| Current Brand Promise | New "Crystallization" Visual |
| :--- | :--- |
| **"Your Expertise"** | Represented by the top glowing cloud (The source). |
| **"Extraction"** | Represented by the vertical drafting lines (The action). |
| **"The Multiplier"** | Represented by the solid, glowing base (The asset). |

### Psychologically:
It tells the founder: *"We aren't just scanning your business; we are mining your brain and building something solid from the raw materials of your vision."*

---
